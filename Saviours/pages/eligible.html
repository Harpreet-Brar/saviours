Eligibility For Donors

A donor should have a gap of three months in their donation process.

The height and age requirements of donors should meet the criteria of donating blood because adults should meet the requirement of having 12- 14 gm of blood in their body.

In some heart conditions, like minor heat attacks or coronary heart disease,
you should not be donating blood for atleast six months.

Women have not been pregnant from last 6 months of donating blood.

You do not have tattoo on your body from last 3 months before donation.

The donors should not have HIV/AIDS , diabetis and other health issues.
